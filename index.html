How it works

01

Read the lesson

Every episode begins with the science you'll need — drugs, physiology, guidelines. Keep the reference book open during the case.

02

Treat the patient

Make decisions like a real clinician. No hints, no feedback during the case — just the patient responding to your choices.

03

Debrief at the end

See every decision you made, what it caused, and what the evidence says. Then go back and try again.

The Differential is a passion project built on the belief that the best way to learn medicine is to feel the weight of a decision before you understand why it matters. Every case is medically accurate. Every outcome is earned.
